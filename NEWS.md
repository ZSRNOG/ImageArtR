# ImageArtR 0.1.0

* Initial development release.
* Added image input normalization, preprocessing, circle packing art, mosaic
  art, outline extraction, sketch/cartoon styles, palette extraction, unified
  S3 methods, saving, and sequential batch processing.
* Added Phase 2A styles: stipple art, halftone art, dedicated hex mosaic, pop
  art panels, duotone images, and ASCII art.
* Added `list_art_styles()`, `list_pop_art_palettes()`, `print_ascii_art()`,
  and `write_ascii_art()`.
* Extended `image_artify()` and `save_image_art()` for Phase 2A styles,
  including TXT and HTML output for ASCII art.
